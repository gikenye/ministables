Frequently Asked Questions

How does Minilend generate returns?
Minilend uses a dual approach: dollar-backed tokens earn yield through Aave integration, while other stablecoins generate returns through our internal lending pool where borrowers pay interest to lenders.
What makes Minilend unique?
Minilend allows you to use your savings as a security to take a quick loan in your local currency while maintaining your savings in dollars to reduce currency devaluation.
Is my money safe with Minilend?
Yes. Minilend uses battle-tested smart contracts, integrates with Aave for additional security, and maintains over-collateralization ratios. All transactions are transparent on the Celo blockchain.
How quickly can I access my funds?
Funds can be withdrawn immediately after the lock period ends. Borrowing is instant once you've deposited collateral. Emergency withdrawals may be available depending on pool liquidity.
What is zkSelf compliance?
zkSelf enables privacy-preserving KYC verification using zero-knowledge proofs. You can prove your identity and compliance status without revealing personal information, meeting regulatory requirements while protecting your privacy.
